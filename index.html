Extra dimension

Walking all the way back toward a start,
being convoluted,
running fast into a wall,
growling to nag,
screaming loudly to find a rhythm,
drinking a whole emptiness,
wallowing in the nostalgia,
suffering from the lack of noise,
hugging paranoia,
lightening up the whole nervous system,

we need an extra dimension ...

It's about to happen!

Story begins when it happens.
It happens when life for a very short time scale goes straight than circular.
The life goes straight when there is a single epiphenomenon that hates his phenomenon.
He hates his phenomenon when phenomena are alienated from the society.
The alienation emerges when structures devour the existence.
The existence is fucked up when the time does not capture all degrees of freedom.
It does not capture them when the dimensionality collapses inside.
It collapses inside, when a very tiny portion of regularity can be seen in the chaos.

multiverse

Sitting in a train,
heading toward south,
north,
east,
west.

Outside is all white; snow everywhere,
the window steams up with my breathe,
foggy window,
I wipe it out;
the window never fogs up twice.

A creepy man in front of me,
he seems like a retired pedophile
who misses the good old days.
Coffee, coffee,
a train crew passed.
“With a shot of rum, please” I say.
Ding, ding, ding,
“Good evening ladies and gentlemen,
we will shortly be arriving at the station Limbo.
The time goes straight forward.
The space is not bended.”

The creepy man leaves.

The train rides forward.

Fingertips on the skin,
caress,
cuddle,
gentle,
riding for a little.

Heading north,
her eyes,
her lips,
she smiles.

Heading south,
whimpering ohhs and ahhs.

Riding with more vigor.

Ding, ding, ding,
“Good night ladies and gentlemen,
we will shortly be twisting in the expansion of the space.
The fabric of space-time will be warped.”

The train rides forward.

The coffee splashes on my face,
shivering, shaking.

The twisted void …
The twisted void …
The twisted void …

Outside is not all white anymore,
at least 100 colors,
what is there outside?
seems like:
the intimacy hidden behind bunch of cells,
the beauty lost in machismo,
the charm of happiness through convoluted essences,
the pulchritude in the connected lives.

Memory: an orgy with many “I”s

An open door,
empty room with a big hole on one of the walls,
inside the hole just darkness,
absolute nothingness ...

Where am I?
It is me!
Is it my experiencing self or remembering self?
It is happening ...

Small creatures appear everywhere,
going in every direction,
seems never heard of gravity.
They are playing me,
entire me ...

My Id, ideal ego, super ego,
what a chaos ...

How could I be me?
I love me, I hate me,
I am going to destroy me ...

Some of the creatures vanishing in the dark hole,
the chaos finds some structures, some orders:
A big orgy with many “I”s,
I am giving a birth to more “I”s ...

Struggling “memory-dominant” era:
Passed my mid-life long ago,
Having longer memories than
time left to my shut down ...
